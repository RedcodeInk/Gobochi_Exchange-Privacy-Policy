To Delete your account from our database, Follow these steps.

1.   Open the app
2.   Go to settings
        - There will be a prompt asking if you really want to delete your account(This action is irreversable)
3.   Enter your password(if you signed up using name and email)
4.   Click Yes(If you want to delete your account from our database

Note: The action of deleting your account is irreversable.
